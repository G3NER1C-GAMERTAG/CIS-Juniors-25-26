HTML Lesson 3: Text Formatting, Ordered & Unordered Lists

Basic Text Formatting

Use the strong tag or the b tag to make something bold.

The Difference Between Strong and B

The Strong tag is an HTML Semantic tag. There is a meaning behind using the Strong tag. What that meaning is: Tells the browser there is something important about the text inside that tag.

The B tag, means nothing. It simply bolds text.

The em Tag and the i Tag

The em Tag and the i Tag, make the text inside of them italic. Again, the difference is, the em Tag is a semantic.

The u tag

The u tag simply underlines your text.

HTML Lists
